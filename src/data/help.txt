Welcome to caleb's facial recognition blockchain system.

How to use:
- The console displays feedback form the program
- The program tries to connect to the rest of the network automaticlly
- To create an "identity", simply click "create new identity" on the
main menu and follow the procedure by taking a picture of your face 
while the camera feedback shows a box around your face, this means 
the program detects your face
- To view what identitys are on the ledger, simply click "open ledger" on 
the main menu
